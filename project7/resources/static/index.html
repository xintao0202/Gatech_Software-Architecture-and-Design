About the Course Management System

Administrators can use the CS6310 Course Management System as a means to add new students, courses, and instructors to the system. Additionally, use the Administrator dashboard to view the current semester and begin the next semester.

Students can find themselves by searching by their UUID on the Student dashboard. Students will be able to view their academic record, currently enrolled courses, and waitlisted courses. Finally, students can request course enrollment through the Request Course feature.

Instructors can also find themselves by searching by UUID on the Instructor dashboard. Instructors are welcome to search for courses to teach, view courses currently being taught, and assigning grades to student records.

For statistics on popular courses and instructors, as well as course suggestions, visit the Weka page.

Use the Initialize Data tool with caution. All current data will be lost and reloaded from an initial data snapshot.
